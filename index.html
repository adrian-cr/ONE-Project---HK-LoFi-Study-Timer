How It Works This timer works in cycles (masks) of 4 intervals (shards).
Each shard takes 25 minutes of focused work to obtain. Once a shard is obtained, you get a 5-minute break. Once 4 shards are obtained, a mask is completed, and you get a 30-minute break.  =   Complete as many masks as you need!
